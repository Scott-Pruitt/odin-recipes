Sausage Balls

These sausage balls are a delicious and satisfying addition to your favorite meal. They're perfect for those who love a good crispy crust and a satisfying filling. I've been making them for years, and I can't wait to share them with everyone!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground sausage
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated feta cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground sausage, Parmesan, mozzarella, feta, cheddar, Swiss, Monterey Jack, and any other optional cheeses.
  2. Form the sausage mixture into sausage balls. You can do this by pressing the sausage into a ball shape with a spoon, then rolling it up to form the balls. Place the balls on a floured surface and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
